Why I switched from Pandas to Polars for large datasets
Nov 18, 2025 // Performance Tuning

# The Pandas way (slow)
# df = pd.read_csv("heavy_data.csv")

# The Polars way (fast)

  .filter(pl.col("amount") > 100))
df = q.collect()
