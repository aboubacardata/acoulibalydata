The Business Need

Overview

The hotel chain needed a comprehensive solution to move beyond basic reporting and gain deep insights into their revenue streams. Management required a tool to make data-driven decisions about pricing, occupancy, marketing strategies, and customer segmentation to maximize profitability.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Revenue Leakage: High cancellation rates were going unnoticed, leading to lost revenue opportunities.
  • Segment Performance: Difficulty in comparing and optimizing performance between City and Resort locations.
  • Pricing Strategy: Lack of granular data prevented dynamic pricing adjustments based on demand.
  • Marketing ROI: Inefficient targeting of customer segments (Corporate, OTA, etc.) reduced marketing effectiveness.
