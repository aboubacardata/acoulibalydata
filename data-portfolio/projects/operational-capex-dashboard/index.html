The Business Need

Overview

Operations, finance, and procurement teams needed a way to effectively monitor capital expenditure trends, project allocations, and investment effectiveness. The goal was to move away from manual reconciliation and gain visibility into spending shifts.

Key Challenges Addressed

  • Visibility: Lack of clear visibility into capital investments and spending shifts.
  • Efficiency: Manual reconciliation of CapEx data was time-consuming and error-prone.
  • Optimization: Difficulty in identifying opportunities for cost savings and efficiency improvements.
  • Governance: Need to flag uncategorized spend and missing project information.
